Air Fryer French Toast

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup butter pecan-flavored coffee creamer
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 slices brioche bread
  • nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F.
  • Whip together coffee creamer, eggs, and salt in a bowl
  • Dip each slice into mixture, shake off excess
  • Spray the air fryer basket with nonstick spray. Place bread in the basket, making sure they don't overlap
  • Reduce air fryer to 350 degrees and fry French toast for 7 minutes, flip halfway.
  • Keep cooked French toast warm in oven.
