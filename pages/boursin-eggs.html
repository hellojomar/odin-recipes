Boursin Eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • ¼ cup half and half
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • ¼ wedge Boursin cheese
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Crack 4 eggs into medium bowl. Pour half and half, then add sea salt.
  2. Let egg mixture sit 5 minutes, up to 15 minutes for creamiest texture.
  3. Heat small non-stick skillet over medium heat. When skillet is warm, add butter and allow to melt until entire skillet is coated
  4. When egg mixture is approximately 50% cooked, add in chunks of Boursin cheese.
  5. Portion scrambled eggs into servings and top with freshly ground black pepper
