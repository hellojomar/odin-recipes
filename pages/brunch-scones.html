Brunch Scones

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 5 tablespoons white sugar
  • ⅔ cup whole milk
  • ½ cup currants
  • 1 egg yolk, beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly grease baking sheet
  2. Sift flour and baking powder into medium bowl. Rub butter and sugar into flour to form a fine crumble. Make a well in center, and add milk and currants.
  3. On a generously floured surface, roll out dough to ¾ inch thickness. Transfer to prepared pan and brush tops with egg yolk.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until risen and lightly golden on top. 12 to 15 minutes
